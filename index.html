About Food Waste

Did you know that one-third of all food produced globally goes to waste?
Food waste contributes to greenhouse gas emissions and depletes valuable resources.
Together, we can take action and make a change.

Simple Tips to Reduce Food Waste

  • Plan your meals and shop smartly.
  • Store food properly to keep it fresh longer.
  • Compost scraps instead of discarding them.
  • Donate surplus food to local charities.
